# Factory Quick-Start Guide

Welcome to China SMM OS — your export growth operating system.

## First Login

1. Open your workspace URL (provided by your account manager)
2. Sign in with the email and password from your welcome email
3. Complete the onboarding wizard at `/onboarding`

## Core Workflows

### 1. Set Up Your Factory Profile
- Navigate to **Factory Platform** — add company info, products, certificates, export markets
- Upload media to **Media Library**

### 2. Content & Publishing
- **Content Factory** — AI-assisted content creation
- **Calendar** — Plan publishing schedule
- **Publishing** — Connect Telegram and schedule posts

### 3. Sales & CRM
- **Customers / Deals / Leads** — Track your sales pipeline
- **Proposals** — Create and send export proposals
- **Deal Room** — Manage active negotiations

### 4. Buyer Network
- **Buyer Discovery** — Find potential buyers
- **Marketplace** — Browse and claim opportunities
- **Outreach** — Contact buyers

### 5. Communications
- **Communication Hub** — Unified inbox
- **WeChat / WhatsApp** — Connect messaging channels (when configured)

### 6. Growth & ROI
- **Growth Center** — Export growth recommendations
- **Customer Success** — ROI and adoption metrics

## Getting Help

- Use **Feedback Center** (`/feedback`) to report bugs, request features, or share suggestions
- Categories: UI, Performance, Content, CRM, Communication, AI, Billing

## Roles

| Role | Typical access |
|------|----------------|
| Owner | Full access including billing and users |
| Manager | Operations, content, sales |
| Sales | CRM, deals, outreach |
| Operator | Content, publishing, inbox |
| Viewer | Read-only dashboards |

## Tips for Pilot Success

- Complete onboarding steps before going live
- Publish at least 3 content items in the first week
- Add your first lead within 14 days
- Review Growth Center weekly
